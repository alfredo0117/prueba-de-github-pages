Mobile Suit Gundam

Facts about Mobile Suit Gundam

Gundam,also known as Gundam Series, is a franchise of animated science fiction series created by Yoshiyuki Tomino for Sunrise Studios.
The theme of the series is war conflicts between humans.
In the series, the warring sides use combat armor called Mobile Suits

Gundam RX78-2

The RX-78-2 Gundam is the titular mobile suit of Mobile Suit Gundam television series. Part of the RX-78 Gundam series, it was built in secret on Side 7.
The Gundam would turn the tide of war in favor of the Earth Federation during the One Year War against the Principality of Zeon. It was primarily piloted by Amuro Ray.
